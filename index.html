Whack-a-Mole!

Welcome to Whack-a-Mole! Here are the rules of the game:

  • When the moles pop up click them as fast as you can!
  • Rounds will last one minute and thirty seconds (1:30)
  • Each round you have 3 lives
  • When you miss, you lose a life and 10 points
  • If your lives are gone before the timer is up the game will end
  • Each time you hit a mole, you get 15 points
